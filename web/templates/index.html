Experiment details

Thank you for agreeing to take part in this experiment. The study is estimated to be 1-hour long. You will be shown videos that you need to rate in terms of beauty from 1-10 where 1 is not beautiful and 10 is extremely beautiful. You will have the option to take a short break after every 15 minutes.  

Your data will be kept pseudo-anonymous and once you have completed and submitted the experiment, you will no longer be able to withdraw your data.
